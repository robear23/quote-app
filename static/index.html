Features

Everything a tradesperson needs

🎙️

Voice Notes

Describe the job out loud on the way to the van. The AI transcribes, structures, and prices it for you.

📸

Photo Capture

Snap a photo of your handwritten notes or a job spec sheet. The AI reads and formats the details automatically.

🧬

Brand DNA

Your business name, address, bank info, and VAT rate are pulled from your existing invoices and applied to every quote.

📄

Word & Excel Output

Get your quotes as .docx or .xlsx — your choice — fully editable and ready to email straight to the client.

✏️

Interactive Refinement

Review a summary before the doc is generated. Change a price, add a line item, or fix a name — all in plain English.

☁️

Quote History

Every quote is saved to your account so you can re-use, reference, or rebill past jobs without digging through paperwork.
